HON. ADEDEJI STANLEY OLAJIDE TO BE HONOURED AS THE PEOPLE-LOVING AND EMPOWERMENT-SENSITIVE LEGISLATOR OF THE YEAR
  • Admin
  • October 5, 2022



Hon. Adedeji Stanley Olajide will be presented with the prestigious award People-Loving and Empowerment-Sensitive Legislator of the Year, for his numerous initiatives and value-added empowerment programs. The award will be presented to him by Celebrology International, the Nigerian global think-tank that is dedicated to the intellectual pursuits of human dignity, celebrating the dignity of leadership.

The award is expected to be presented to him at his constituency office by the think-tank on Friday, 7th of October, 2022 in commemoration of Nigeria's Independence Day at 62. Celebrology International remains the No. 1 global think-tank with a Nigerian root, pioneering discoveries on celebration. Using the template of numerous irepedic, patriotic and other classes of celebration in celebrating people with awards, the organisation considers Hon. Adedeji Stanley Olajide, the true Ibadan son worthy to receive its award.

The great Information Technologist won the March 2019 general election with a margin of 2,800 votes to defeat his opponent from the All Progressive Party. He was sworn in into the House of Representatives, National Assembly to represent the good people of Ibadan South-West/ North-West Federal Constituency, Ibadan, Oyo State.

The 9th Assembly was inaugurated on Tuesday 11th June 2019. Rep. Adedeji Stanley Olajide has proved himself to be a good ambassador of his constituents. He hits the ground running on the exact day of his inauguration. At the inaugural sitting, he moved his first motion on Matters of Urgent Public Importance on the “Need to Combat Threatening Environmental Degradation across the Country”. He put a prayer along with the motion to include Oluyole Industrial Park, Oluyole Estate, College Crescent, Anfani, Bolumole, Eleyele, Olopomewa, Odo-Ona, and Ogunpa –all under Ibadan Southwest and Northwest Local Government in the list that Ecological Fund Office should address.

Rep. Adedeji Stanley Olajide – Odidi Omo’s Notable Achievements in Not Less Than 2 Years in Office. He has sponsored several motions, move series of Bills. He is an active member of both the Standing Committees and Ad-hoc Committees of the House of Representatives.

Adedeji Stanley Olajide. Honorable Member – Federal House of Representatives representing Ibadan South West/ North West Constituency, has shown genuine political leadership trait and deserves some accolades for his jaw-dropping achievements.

These achievements may not be reported, underreported, or not celebrated at all by disco critiques and opposition who are in every way not ready to give credits to him for the achievements but rather criticize. But for the sake of record purpose. I will like to educate and inform the general public by highlighting some of the achievements of Rep. Adedeji Stanley Olajide starting with the provision of 300KVA transformer for the residents of Kuola, Ward 12 Ibadan South West, and Aba Igbira at Omololu Olunloyo Estate, to put an end to their electricity challenges.

He initiated 3 Days Free Medical Outreach to his constituency residents’ that were having health issues. As a member of various Committees in the House, He has sponsored and co-sponsored many Bills in the House of Representatives.

As a man of his word and a promise keeper, he gifted out luxury cars to his Constituency Coordinators, gave out a luxury car to Lebanon Street Mosques Imam at the Old Gbagi Market. He Donated an appreciable sum of money towards Church development within the Constituency.

As if all that is not enough, He reeled out in batches, several COVID-19 Relief Packages to the residents during the early spike of Covid-19 that cost hundreds of millions of naira.

He gave out an appreciable educational grant to pupils in his Constituency. He is an exemplary representative, leader, and an extraordinary man who craves for development and wellbeing of his people (He is truly here to serve and that is his priority). As security is No. 1 in any society. Rep. Adedeji embarks on the installation of Street Lights on the streets of Idi-ikan, Ori Eru, Anfani, Ajeigbe, Adeoyo, Imale Falafia, College Crescent, and so on.

He also Drills Boreholes in various locations within the constituency and plans to drill one borehole each in every ward of Ibadan North West & South West, after seeing access to water hardship that is being experienced by the residents.

Rep. Adedeji is a leader who knows the importance of education and ready to improve the system of education in his constituency. He Renovated some dilapidated classrooms in schools like Ratibi Primary School, UMC Oke Ado, St. Patrick School and provided modern tables and chairs that numbered 970 into the classrooms. Also empowered the students with ICT programs in the schools like Baptist High School, Oke-Ado in Southwest, and Eleyele Secondary School in Northwest. Providing computers for students with the interest of learning ICT.

Furthermore, he empowered youths by creating an avenue to learn Paint Making & Marketing * and gave them money to start up the business and also created *Fishery Opportunity in collaboration with the federal ministry of agriculture in Abuja. And equipped them with the necessary materials to get them started which also included start-up capital.

Nevertheless, as a great leader with foresight, who knows the importance of eating protein and foresaw the high level of meat consumption in our society, he thereby empowered the community in his constituency to learn Animal Husbandry.

He affiliated with the Federal Ministry of Agriculture And Rural Development of animal husbandry services to present community empowerment through animal husbandry development.

To mention but a few from the achievements of Rep. Adedeji who is still touching the lives of his constituency people positively and he is not resting on his oars, as he still has a lot of transformational development program to bring up. Watch out for this great extraordinary man. It is truly a blessing to have you in our time. What he has done is just the tip of the iceberg from the good plans that he has in mind for the good people of his Constituency and Oyo State at large
