CHIEF MRS MODUPE JOHNSON BAGS AWARD AS JEWEL OF INESTIMABLE VALUE
  • Admin
  • September 22, 2022

The PDP candidate for the Lagos State House of Assembly, representing Surulere State Constituency II, Chief Mrs Modupe Johnson popularly known as godmother has bagged an award from Celebrology International as Jewel of Inestimable Value.

Jewel of Inestimable Value, best known as JIV, is one of the most prestigious awards from the Nigerian global think-tank that is given to a woman who is yet to hold a public office but is serving the society in appreciating her role for societal upliftment and development through many initiatives and empowerment programs, and Chief Mrs Modupe Johnson from the opposition party, PDP is the first to bag this award in the history of the organisation.

Chief Mrs Modupe Johnson is applauded for good character as one of the iconic women in Lagos for her democratic principles and virtues. Coming from a part of Lagos that believes so much in electing leaders, not on blind allegiance to political party but in respect to personality identity, Chief Mrs Modupe Johnson has added a feather to her cap and is sailing with the ship of popularity to greater heights for her meritorious service and goodwill to humanity.
