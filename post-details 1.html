WHO IS SOLA ODUMOSU, Father of Celebrology?

From the Cradle, Through The Thinking Radicalisation to the birth of Celebrology.
  • Admin
  • July 7, 2022

Sola Odumosu ranks among the world founding fathers of knowledge and theorists the world has ever known. He is the first black man African descent, to create a globally acclaimed academic body of knowledge known as Celebrology- an academic discipline on ' human dignity', that stands shoulder to shoulder with the likes of Philosophy, Sociology, Anthropology, Psychology and others. He is also the first person in the history of global civilization to propound the celebration theory, which has apparently conferred on him the greatest intellectual responsibility as the World Leading Celebration Thinker, leading in many global discoveries on celebration. Sola Odumosu also championed several discoveries, postulated theories within the intellectual parameter of Celebrology such as: theory of heroism, theory of immortalism, theory of legendarism, theory of majemrarey, theory of keroepaporh and the theory of conferrism.

Sola Odumosu is the Nigerian-born intellectual genius. He was born on the 4th of May, 1982 at the General Hospital, Ikeja, where his mother, Felicia Mosunmola was a midwife. Mosunmola Odumosu suffered intensely from stillbirth and was often confronted with ill- health problems. His father’s name is Joseph Odumosu – a tailor from Odosenbora in Ijebu kingdom. The family has a long standing lineage from Ijebu-Ode, the cradle of Ijebu civilization. His patriarchs were known for their pioneering movement of Christianity in Ijebuland in Africa, he is said to have inherited the gene of invention and intellectual deviance from his family.
