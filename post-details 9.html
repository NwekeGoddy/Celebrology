ENGR SALIU YUSUF HOSTS IMCOSA SET '94 AMIDST ILEYA FESTIVAL
  • Admin
  • July 10, 2022



In what appears as get together event for the Ijebu Muslim College Old Students' Association (IMCOSA) just on the second day of Eid-el-Kabir, Sunday 10th July, 2022, just a day to the popular Ojude Oba Festival, held in his Ososa residence, perhaps the first general meeting of the new executives, an Ilustrious member of the set, Engr. Saliu Yusuf hosts IMCOSA Set 94.

The meeting started with an opening prayer by a member of the set in person of Mr Nurudeen Adeyemi. Following the prayer, the President, Mr Zubair Mubarak Olaoluwapo sprang into action charging the members on various things and what should be done by each member in moving the set forward and the contribution of the Set to its alma mater.

The President opened the floor to members for contribution. Mr Jamiu Olawale Edu was the first to speak. He stressed on the welfare of the members. The Head Boy of the Set, Mr Alexander Idowu spoke on the need for secretariat for the set, emphasing it that it boosts the future hostage of members.
Mrs Adekoya Fausat Adetutu buttressed the need for welfare of the members again. Mrs Alausa Atinuke spoke on the need for contributions of members for the set's development and the presence of the executives at every meeting, re-emphasizing her earlier contribution to the set's active participation. Mr Yusuf Ahmed Abiodun spoke on the unity of members, stressing on ways members can help each other. Mr Bisoye Olukoya talked on welfare of members, stressing the need for love among members of the set. There were reactions from members

The President of the set, Mr Zubair Mubarak Olaoluwapo responded by quickly appointing Project Committee to be chaired by Mr Bisoye Olukoya, while the Welfare Committee is to be headed by Mr Dina Abiodun who were both initial contributors to the discussions at the meeting. The meeting which was held for about two hours, was brought to a close by Mr Alexander Idowu, the old boy of the set at about 5pm.This year's hostage by the Ososa-born indigene, in the person of Engr. Saliu Yusuf, which is tagged: IMCOSA SET '94 ILEYA GALORE held in his residence on Sunday 10th July, 2022 was historic as it marked a turning point in the development of IMCOSA '94 set.
